Deposit theft

Players make deposits, but their funds never show up in their accounts. Or, they may receive a notification that their deposit was declined.

Non-payment

Online gambling scams can take the form of casinos that don’t pay out. Here, the player wins money, but the site claims they don’t meet the wagering requirements for withdrawal, even if they do.

Rigged games

Some scammers create fake online casinos and rig the games so you can never win. By doing this, they encourage you to keep playing (and losing) in the hopes that you’ll eventually give up or deposit even more money.
